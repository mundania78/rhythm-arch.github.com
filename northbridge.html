Northbridge Plaza Renovations

The existing plaza, designed in the 1980’s was quite dated and involved a much-needed facelift.

The project has gone through several iterations, including a neo-colonial style, a post-modern plaza style, a retro-fit of existing conditions, as well as a total reconstruction of the building façade. Working with contractors and engineers on budgetary constraints allowed the project to be developed in these multiple styles aiding the owner to envision the future of the shopping center.

The final style incorporated pieces of several different iterations, modernizing the building to attract tenants and customers, while differentiating itself from similar shopping centers in the area.
